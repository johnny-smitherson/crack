- cosmetic wheels
  - the cars' wheels should be procedurally placed only cosmetically by taking the values of the car's orientation, of the point and surface of contact with the ground. The wheels are simple black cylinders with the usual black/white debug texture. Use the car's velocity and the wheel's diameter to compute wheel rotation, and use the hit with the ground to compute wheel position. The cosmetic wheel does not have any physics role at all. Its position will be computed from the car data in the Last schedule to make sure we render them in the correct place at all times.
